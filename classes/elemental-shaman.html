Elemental Shaman

Caster • Utility Master • Purge Specialist

Master of utility with Purge trains, Hex + Ascendance burst, and perfect utility rotations. Defines fights with Purge/Hex + Ascendance burst.

R1 Behaviors & Coaching Points

Positioning

  • Midline positioning: Between team and backline
  • Pre-positioning: Always ready for swaps
  • Totem placement: Perfect positioning for team
  • Defensive positioning: Safe from pressure

Utility Mastery

  • Kick discipline: Kicks enemy healer on CD
  • NS Hex sequencing: After trinkets/dispels
  • Purge bot: Strips defensives proactively
  • Totem rotation: Perfect timing and placement

Burst Execution

  • Ascendance timing: Perfect burst windows
  • Hex coordination: Perfect with team stuns
  • Purge trains: Strips enemy defensives
  • Resource management: Perfect mana control

Team Leadership

  • Utility calling: Leads Purge/Hex chains
  • Totem coordination: Perfect team synergy
  • Strategic thinking: Leads team's utility strategy
  • Playmaking: Creates game-winning opportunities

Key Abilities & Usage

Purge

Your primary utility tool. Use to strip enemy defensives and create offensive pressure.

R1 Tips: Lead Purge trains, coordinate with team, strip defensives

Hex

Your CC tool. Use on healers and coordinate with team stuns for perfect CC rotations.

R1 Tips: Hex healer in swaps, coordinate with team, perfect timing

Ascendance

Your burst tool. Use during kill windows to create massive pressure and force cooldowns.

R1 Tips: Save for swaps, coordinate with team, perfect timing

Totems

Your utility tools. Use Tremor, Grounding, and other totems to counter enemy CC and support team.

R1 Tips: Perfect timing, team coordination, counter enemy CC
