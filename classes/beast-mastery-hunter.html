Key Abilities & Usage

Bestial Wrath

Your primary burst tool. Use during kill windows to create massive pressure and force cooldowns.

R1 Tips: Save for swaps, coordinate with team, perfect timing

Pet Control

Your control tool. Use pet abilities to control positioning and create pressure.

R1 Tips: Perfect pet positioning, team coordination, optimal control

Deterrence

Your defensive tool. Use proactively before incoming pressure, not reactively.

R1 Tips: Pre-use before swaps, coordinate with healers, never waste

Disengage

Your mobility tool. Use to engage, escape, and position for optimal ranged.

R1 Tips: Perfect positioning, escape pressure, engage optimally
