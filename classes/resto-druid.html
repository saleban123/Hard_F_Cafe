Resto Druid

Healer • Hot Specialist • Mobility Master

Master of healing over time with Lifebloom, Rejuvenation, and Wild Growth. The ultimate mobile healer with perfect positioning and utility support.

Progression Guide

Master Resto Druid from 1300 rating to R1 with these key improvements at each bracket

1300-1400

Foundation

Key Focus: Learn basic healing rotation and Hot management

  • Master Lifebloom, Rejuvenation, Wild Growth
  • Understand Hot stacking and management
  • Learn basic positioning fundamentals
1400-1500

Mobility

Key Focus: Master cat form and travel form positioning

  • Learn to use cat form for mobility
  • Master travel form escapes
  • Understand when to shift forms
1500-1600

Utility Usage

Key Focus: Use Innervate and Nature's Swiftness effectively

  • Master Innervate timing for mana management
  • Learn Nature's Swiftness clutch heals
  • Understand utility cooldown management
1600-1700

Tranquility Usage

Key Focus: Master Tranquility timing and positioning

  • Master positioning for maximum effect

  • Learn Wild Growth timing for team heals

R1 Behaviors & Coaching Points

Positioning

  • Anchor positioning: Backline, never frontline
  • Pre-positioning: Always ready for swaps
  • Max range: Perfect positioning for CC
  • Defensive positioning: Safe from pressure

CC Mastery

  • Cyclone engine: Off-healer + CC bot, rotates Cyclones flawlessly
  • Beam + Vortex synergy: Chains with Boomkin
  • Pre-positioning: Max range before swaps
  • DR tracking: Perfect Cyclone DR management

Healing Mastery

  • Mana management: Drinks constantly, never OOM
  • CD coordination: Perfect with healers
  • Off-healing: Clutch heals when needed
  • Resource management: Perfect mana control

Team Leadership

  • CC calling: Leads team's CC strategy
  • Combo coordination: Perfect team synergy
  • Strategic thinking: Leads team's CC strategy
  • Playmaking: Creates game-winning opportunities

Key Abilities & Usage

Lifebloom

Your primary healing tool. Keep rolling on tanks and key targets for maximum healing output.

R1 Tips: Perfect uptime, roll on tanks, coordinate with team

Rejuvenation

Your main Hot. Use on all teammates for consistent healing and mana efficiency.

R1 Tips: Perfect uptime, mana efficiency, team coverage

Wild Growth

Your team healing tool. Use during team fights for maximum healing coverage.

R1 Tips: Perfect timing, team coordination, maximum coverage

Tranquility

Your major cooldown. Use during intense team fights for massive healing output.

R1 Tips: Perfect timing, coordinate with healers, maximum effect
