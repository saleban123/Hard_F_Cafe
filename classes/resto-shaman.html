R1 Behaviors & Coaching Points

Positioning

  • Anchor positioning: Backline, never frontline
  • Pre-positioning: Always ready for swaps
  • Totem placement: Perfect positioning for team
  • Defensive positioning: Safe from pressure

Totem Mastery

  • Totem rotation: Tremor on Fear, Grounding on Polys, Cap for peels
  • AMZ anchor: Spirit Link always on swaps, never random
  • Purge trains: Strips enemy defensives proactively
  • Hex off targets: Hex healer on team swaps

Utility Mastery

  • Purge mastery: Strips defensives proactively
  • Hex timing: Perfect with team stuns
  • Totem coordination: Perfect team synergy
  • Resource management: Perfect mana control

Team Leadership

  • Defensive caller: Leads team's defensive strategy
  • Utility coordination: Perfect team synergy
  • Strategic thinking: Leads team's utility strategy
  • Playmaking: Creates game-winning opportunities

Key Abilities & Usage

Spirit Link Totem

Your team defensive tool. Use on swaps to save the entire team, never randomly.

R1 Tips: Always on swaps, never random, coordinate with team

Purge

Your offensive utility tool. Use to strip enemy defensives and create pressure.

R1 Tips: Strips defensives proactively, coordinate with team, create pressure

Hex

Your CC tool. Use on healers and coordinate with team stuns for perfect CC rotations.

R1 Tips: Hex healer on team swaps, coordinate with team, perfect timing

Totems

Your utility tools. Use Tremor, Grounding, and other totems to counter enemy CC and support team.

R1 Tips: Perfect timing, team coordination, counter enemy CC
