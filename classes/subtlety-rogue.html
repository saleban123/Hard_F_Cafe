Key Abilities & Usage

Sap

Your primary CC tool. Use on healers and chain with team stuns for perfect CC rotations.

R1 Tips: Sap healer before every burst go, coordinate with team

Shadow Dance

Your burst tool. Use during kill windows to create massive pressure and force cooldowns.

R1 Tips: Save for swaps, coordinate with team, perfect timing

Evasion

Your defensive tool. Use proactively before incoming pressure, not reactively.

R1 Tips: Pre-use before swaps, coordinate with healers, never waste

Stealth

Your positioning tool. Use to engage, escape, and position for optimal CC.

R1 Tips: Perfect positioning, escape pressure, engage optimally
