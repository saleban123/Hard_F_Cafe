Holy Paladin

Healer • Defensive Leader • Utility Master

The ultimate defensive coordinator with Sac, Bop, and Clemency management. Leads entire team's defensive rotation and calls all major cooldowns.

Progression Guide

Master Holy Paladin from 1300 rating to R1 with these key improvements at each bracket

1300-1400

Foundation

Key Focus: Learn to use Sac, Bop, Freedom on teammates

  • Master basic utility usage
  • Learn positioning fundamentals
  • Understand cooldown management
1400-1500

Utility Timing

Key Focus: Save Sac/Bop for swaps, not random

  • Time utility with team needs
  • Learn to read incoming pressure
  • Coordinate with other healers
1500-1600

Healing Mastery

Key Focus: Use WoG/FoL to peel teammates

  • Master instant healing spells
  • Learn to peel with heals
  • Understand mana management
1600-1700

CD Coordination

Key Focus: Time Clemency CDs smartly

  • Coordinate with team cooldowns
  • Learn to stagger defensives
  • Master bubble usage
1700-1800

Bubble Discipline

Key Focus: Save bubble only for yourself

  • Never waste bubble on teammates
  • Learn to position for swaps
  • Master defensive positioning
1800-1900

Offensive Utility

Key Focus: HoJ healers in swaps

  • Use HoJ offensively
  • Coordinate with team swaps
  • Learn to call targets
1900-2000

Burst Coordination

Key Focus: Line wings with team bursts

  • Coordinate burst windows
  • Learn to read teamfight flow
  • Master CD timing
2000-2100

Team Leadership

Key Focus: Call Sac/Bop for team

  • Lead defensive rotations
  • Call out incoming pressure
  • Coordinate with all teammates
2100-2200

Perfect Timing

Key Focus: Balance burst + utility

  • Perfect balance of offense and defense
  • Master all utility spells
  • Learn to read all situations
2200-2300

Utility Mastery

Key Focus: Perfect utility rotation

  • Flawless utility execution
  • Perfect CD management
  • Master all advanced techniques
2300-2400

Offensive Leadership

Key Focus: More aggressive HoJ setups

  • Lead offensive utility usage
  • Create kill opportunities
  • Master all playmaking abilities
2400-2500

Team Coordination

Key Focus: Lead support CD calls

  • Lead entire team's defensive strategy
  • Coordinate all support cooldowns
  • Master team leadership
2500-2600 R1

R1 Mastery

Key Focus: Turns HPal into utility powerhouse, always clutch

  • Flawless execution in all situations
  • Leads entire team's defensive strategy
  • Creates game-winning plays
  • Perfect balance of offense and defense

R1 Behaviors & Coaching Points

Positioning

  • Anchor positioning: Midline, never frontline
  • Pre-positioning: Always ready for swaps
  • Bubble discipline: Only for yourself
  • Defensive positioning: Behind team, safe from pressure

Utility Management

  • Pre-Sac swaps: Sac before CC, not after
  • Clemency management: Double Freedom/Bop proactively
  • HoJ chains: HoJ healer every kill go
  • Call rotations: Leads entire team's defensive rotation

Healing Mastery

  • WoG/FoL usage: Peel teammates with heals
  • Mana management: Never OOM at R1
  • CD coordination: Perfect timing with team
  • Burst healing: Wings + team burst coordination

Team Leadership

  • Defensive caller: Calls entire team's defensive rotation
  • CD coordination: Leads all support cooldowns
  • Target calling: Calls out incoming pressure
  • Playmaker role: Creates game-winning opportunities

Key Abilities & Usage

Hand of Sacrifice

Your primary defensive utility. Use on teammates under pressure, especially during swaps.

R1 Tips: Pre-Sac swaps, coordinate with team, never waste

Hand of Protection

Physical immunity for teammates. Use on melee under pressure or to prevent kills.

R1 Tips: Save for swaps, coordinate with Clemency, call usage

Hammer of Justice

Your offensive CC tool. Use on healers during swaps to create kill opportunities.

R1 Tips: HoJ healer every kill go, coordinate with team stuns

Divine Shield

Your personal immunity. Use only when absolutely necessary, never on teammates.

R1 Tips: Save for yourself only, pre-position > bubble, coordinate with healers
