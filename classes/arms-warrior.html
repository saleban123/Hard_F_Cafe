R1 Behaviors & Coaching Points

Positioning

  • Anchor positioning: Midline, never frontline
  • Pre-positioning: Always ready for swaps
  • Burst positioning: Perfect for Bladestorm
  • Defensive positioning: Safe from pressure

Burst Execution

  • Bladestorm timing: Perfect with team stuns
  • Fear chains: Fear healer before every burst go
  • CC coordination: Perfect with team CC
  • Resource management: Perfect rage control

Team Coordination

  • Burst coordination: Perfect timing with team
  • CC chains: Works with all teammates
  • Call rotations: Leads team burst strategy
  • Strategic thinking: Leads team's burst strategy

Defensive Play

  • Defensive Stance discipline: Perfect timing
  • Kiting mastery: Never dies to ranged
  • Defensive usage: Never dies with defensives unused
  • Resource management: Perfect rage control

Key Abilities & Usage

Bladestorm

Your primary burst tool. Use during kill windows to create massive pressure and force cooldowns.

R1 Tips: Save for swaps, coordinate with team, perfect timing

Fear

Your CC tool. Use on healers and chain with team stuns for perfect CC rotations.

R1 Tips: Fear healer before every burst go, coordinate with team

Defensive Stance

Your defensive tool. Use proactively before incoming pressure, not reactively.

R1 Tips: Pre-use before swaps, coordinate with healers, never waste

Charge

Your mobility tool. Use to engage, escape, and position for optimal burst.

R1 Tips: Perfect positioning, escape pressure, engage optimally
