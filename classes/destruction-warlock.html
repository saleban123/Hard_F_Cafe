Destruction Warlock

Caster • Burst Specialist • Chaos Bolt Master

Master of burst damage with Chaos Bolt setups, Shadowfury coordination, and Fear chains. The ultimate burst playmaker for RBG teams.

R1 Behaviors & Coaching Points

Positioning

  • Anchor positioning: Max range, never frontline
  • Pre-positioning: Always ready for swaps
  • Kiting mastery: Perfect escape timing
  • Defensive positioning: Safe from pressure

Burst Execution

  • CB setups: Chaos Bolts only inside stun/CC
  • Fear discipline: Fear healer before every CB go
  • Shadowfury anchor: Sets up kills with AoE stun
  • Ember management: Perfect resource control

Team Coordination

  • Burst coordination: Perfect timing with team
  • Setup calling: Leads team burst strategy
  • CC chains: Works with all teammates
  • Call rotations: Leads team burst strategy

Defensive Play

  • Dark Bargain discipline: Perfect timing
  • Kiting mastery: Never dies to melee
  • Defensive usage: Never dies with defensives unused
  • Resource management: Perfect Ember control

Key Abilities & Usage

Chaos Bolt

Your primary burst tool. Only use when you can guarantee the kill or force major cooldowns.

R1 Tips: Save for team goes, coordinate with stuns, never waste

Shadowfury

Your AoE stun tool. Use to set up kills with team coordination and create burst windows.

R1 Tips: Perfect timing, team coordination, burst setup

Fear

Your CC tool. Use on healers before every burst go and chain with team stuns.

R1 Tips: Fear healer before every CB go, coordinate with team

Dark Bargain

Your defensive cooldown. Use proactively before incoming pressure, not reactively.

R1 Tips: Pre-use before swaps, coordinate with healers, never waste
