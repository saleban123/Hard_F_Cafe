Key Abilities & Usage

Fear

Your primary CC tool. Use on healers constantly, chain with team stuns, and control the battlefield.

R1 Tips: Call out targets, track DRs, use defensively to peel

DoT Spread

Your pressure engine. Keep DoTs on all targets, use Soul Swap to spread, and maintain constant pressure.

R1 Tips: Perfect uptime, strategic spreading, pressure control

Gateway

Team mobility tool. Place strategically for team positioning and escape routes.

R1 Tips: Perfect placement, team coordination, strategic positioning
